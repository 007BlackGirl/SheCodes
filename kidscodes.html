Teaching coding skills to young children!

KidsCodes is a coding platform that teaches young children between the ages of 7 - 16 years old to code. Children and teenagers learn block-based and text-based coding through an engaging game-like environment. Millions of KidsCodes's students are now excited about coding! KidsCodes does not require prior coding experience to teach and is designed for schools, clubs, and home use.
